Ultrawide Snowboards

Those of us with big feet (I'm a US 13) know that toe and heel drag is a big issue, especially if you want to do Knapton style carves.

An ultrawide board is one that's over 28cm wide at the waist. Most boards are aronud 25cm, "wide" boards are typically around 26.5cm.

Finding a big board is tough. Most manufacturers don't make ultrawide boards, and those that do bury the boards.

This website is a list of every ultrawide board I've found. Since almost every board comes in multiple sizes, I'm only listing the widest and longest variant. You'd never ride anything smaller, right?

If you're not 6'2" with a size 13 foot or larger this site is not for you.

Some people like to overcome a narrow board by widening their stance, angling their feet more, getting risers, or just ignoring it. I'm of a different mind: I think a wider board will let you ride more naturally.
